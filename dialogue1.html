Haere ki te Whare Kararehe — Dialogue (flip each card)

Tap a card to flip and see the English translation on the back. One card per row — simple sentences for Year 5.

Hemi:Kia ora, e Aroha! Kei te haere koe ki te whare kararehe?(tap)
Hi, Aroha! Are you going to the zoo?
Aroha:Āe, kei te haere au!
Yes, I’m going!
Hemi:Ka haere tāua āpōpō?
Shall we go tomorrow?
Aroha:Āe, ka pai!
Yes, that’s good!
Hemi:Kei te hiahia au ki te kite i te raiona.
I want to see the lion.
Aroha:Kei te hiahia au ki ngā makimaki.
I like the monkeys.
Hemi:He ngahau ngā makimaki!
The monkeys are funny!
Aroha:Āe, he tino ngahau.
Yes, they are really funny.
Hemi:Ka kawe au i te wai.
I’ll bring the water.
Aroha:Ka kawe au i ngā kai.
I’ll bring the food.
Hemi:Ka pai!
Good!
Aroha:Kei te hiahia hoki au ki te tango whakaahua.
I also want to take photos.
Hemi:Me tango whakaahua tāua ki te arewhana.
Let’s take a photo with the elephant.
Aroha:Āe, he nui te arewhana.
Yes, the elephant is big.
Hemi:He ātaahua hoki.
And beautiful too.
Aroha:Ka hoki tāua i te toru karaka.
We’ll go home at three o’clock.
Hemi:Āe, kia pai te rā.
Yes, have a good day.
Aroha:Ka rawe te whare kararehe!
The zoo will be awesome!
Hemi:Āe, ka kite tātou i ngā kararehe katoa.
Yes, we’ll see all the animals.
Aroha:Tino pai!
So good!
Kia pai! Nāu i tino whakamātau — Well done!
