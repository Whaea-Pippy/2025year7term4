Match the sentences — Te Reo & English

Two short matching activities. Tap one Māori sentence and then tap the English sentence you think matches. Each section has 5 sentences.

Te Reo (Set 1)

Aroha: Tēnā koe, e Hemi!
Hemi: Kia ora, e Aroha!
Aroha: Kei te pēhea koe?
Hemi: Kei te pai ahau, ā koe?
Aroha: Kei te tino pai ahau.

English (shuffled)

How are you?
Hello, Hemi!
I’m really good.
Hi, Aroha!
I’m good, and you?
Matches: 0 / 5

Te Reo (Set 2)

Hemi: I pēhea tō hararei?
Aroha: I pai rawa atu! I haere au ki te moana.
Hemi: Ka rawe! I haere hoki koe me tō whānau?
Aroha: Āe, i haere mātou katoa.
Hemi: Tino pai tērā!

English (shuffled)

That’s great!
How was your holiday?
Cool! Did you go with your family?
It was awesome! I went to the beach.
Yes, we all went.
Matches: 0 / 5
