Kaiako: Mā wai te karakia?

Ākonga: Māku te karakia.

Ko te karakia, ko He Hōnore.

Me inoi tātou.

Ko te karakia, ko Kia Tau.

Me inoi tātou.


Leading the Opening Karakia

Kaiako: Mā wai te karakia timatanga?

Ākonga: Māku te karakia timatanga.

Ākonga: Ko te karakia timatanga, ko He Hōnore.

Ākonga: Me inoi tātou.


Teacher: Who will lead the opening karakia?

Student: I will lead the opening karakia.

Student: The opening karakia is He Hōnore.

Student: Let us pray.


Leading the Closing Karakia

Kaiako: Mā wai e ārahi i te karakia whakamutunga?

Ākonga: Māku e ārahi i te karakia whakamutunga.

Ākonga: Ko te karakia whakamutunga, ko Kia Tau.

Ākonga: Me inoi tātou.


Teacher: Who will lead the closing karakia?

Student: I will lead the closing karakia.

Student: The closing karakia is Kia Tau.

Student: Let us pray.
