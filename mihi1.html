Mihi 1 – Kākāno (Beginner)


Whakaara / Introduction

Kia ora tātou te whānau o Hāto Kēneti

Greetings to our Saint Kentigern family.


Karakia / The Prayers

Tēnei te mihi ki te kaikarakia mō ngā karakia whakaritea o tēnei rā.

I would like to acknowledge the person who led the spiritual acknowledgements this day.


Te Whare / The House

Ki te whare e tū mai nei, tū mai, tū mai rā.

The house that stands before me, stand, stand!


Whakaminenga / Gathering

Otirā kia tātou te whānau kua tae mai nei, tēnā tātou katoa.

Also, to the family before me, thank you all.


Te Kaupapa / The Occasion

Kei te mihi hoki ki te kaupapa e whakakao nei a tātou, tēnei ka mihi.

I’d also like to acknowledge the reason that has gathered us all, thank you.


Whakakapi / Conclusion

Nō reira e te whānau, e te kaiako, koutou katoa e āku hoa, tēnā koutou, tēnā koutou, tēnā koutou katoa.

So, to the family, the teacher, all my peers thank you.
